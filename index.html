Since 2005, and in association with Tauranga City Council, more than 20,000 native plants have been planted in Kopurererua Valley, as part of Birds, Bees, Kids and Trees. The hands-on environmental programme educates young people about the importance of preserving nature for the generations to come.

As Paengaroa’s largest employer, Comvita has always been a close supporter of the area’s local school. This has included sponsoring Paengaroa Top School since 2003 and funding a new community garden on school grounds.

Comvita has supported the Otanewainuku Kiwi Trust since its establishment in 2002. Formed by Te Puke Forest and Bird, the trust is focused on growing the brown kiwi population in the Otanewainuku Forest.

Comvita provides Medihoney Antibacterial Wound Gel® to Waipuna Hospice to support their wound care. The hospice, located in Te Puna, provides specialist medical care for patients living with a life-limiting illness and support services for their families.
